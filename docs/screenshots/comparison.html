What changed

  • 380×480 floating window, 6.5pt margin on 393pt width→369×699 full-width window, 12pt margin, 85% viewport height
  • Plain green circle FAB (rbxassetid icon didn't load)→⚡ Unicode glyph, pulsing accent glow, FIXED (never moves on tap)
  • Dragger fired on touch-down (FAB moved when you tried to tap)→8pt drag threshold (iOS HIG) — tap = open, drag = move
  • No visual hierarchy, every row identical, emerald everywhere→Emerald for active, gold for values, red for danger, blue for info, icon column on every row
  • No backdrop, no search, no status bar, no panic button→Backdrop dims game 55%, search in header, status bar with FPS/Ping/Active + ⚠ PANIC
  • Tabs at bottom (cramped 70pt wide, 48pt tall)→Tabs at top (100pt wide, 48pt tall, horizontal scrollable, accent underline)
  • Heavy glass (0.18-0.28 alpha, too opaque over gameplay)→Light glass (0.06-0.10 alpha, see the game through)
  • Toggle ON was emerald fill + emerald knob (no contrast)→Toggle ON is emerald track + WHITE knob (iOS pattern)
  • Mixed easing (Quad/Exponential/Linear scattered)→One easing function everywhere: Quint (Sin for ambient glow only)
  • Panic only via RightCtrl (unreachable on touch)→⚠ PANIC in status bar (44pt, always visible, also RightCtrl)
